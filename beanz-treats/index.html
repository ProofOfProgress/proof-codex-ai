About me

Steven Aaden Castañon

I'm a 17-year-old entrepreneur originally from El Monte, but I moved out to San Bernardino when I was 7. I attended Newmark Elementary from 4th to 6th grade, then transitioned to Shandin Hills Middle School for all of middle school. I then bounced between two high schools before landing at Entrepreneur High. I've moved many times throughout my life and experienced numerous life-changing events, but none of this has ever stopped my dreams of owning my own business and helping my grandma get her house back. That's my goal with this business. Get my grandma her house.

About Beanz Treats

Beanz Treats began in 2021 when, as a middle schooler, I started selling Latin candy out of my backpack. Gradually, I shifted to selling things I made myself and discovered real joy in that process. Sharing my ideas with my grandma and grandpa brought me inspiration and their support. Although my grandpa is no longer here, my grandma still fully supports me. Along with my dad, my sister, my grandparents, and best friend have always meant everything to me. I regret not always being the best grandchild, especially to my grandpa, but hindsight is 20/20. Their support and blessing started this business, and that's why I am determined not to let it fail.
