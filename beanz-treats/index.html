About Me

Steven Aaden Castañon

The person behind Beanz Treats — building something meaningful for the people who believed in him first.

I'm a 17-year-old entrepreneur originally from El Monte, but I moved out to San Bernardino when I was 7. I attended Newmark Elementary from 4th to 6th grade, then transitioned to Shandin Hills Middle School for all of middle school. I then bounced between two high schools before landing at Entrepreneur High.

I've moved many times throughout my life and experienced numerous life-changing events, but none of this has ever stopped my dreams of owning my own business and helping my grandma get her house back. That's my goal with this business. Get my grandma her house.

Every batch is part of a bigger dream — not just selling treats, but building something that lasts for my family.

About Beanz Treats

From backpack sales to homemade joy

What started small became something personal, creative, and full of heart.

How it started

Beanz Treats began in 2021 when, as a middle schooler, I started selling Latin candy out of my backpack. Gradually, I shifted to selling things I made myself and discovered real joy in that process.

Sharing my ideas with my grandma and grandpa brought me inspiration and their support. Although my grandpa is no longer here, my grandma still fully supports me.

Why it matters

Along with my dad, my sister, my grandparents, and best friend have always meant everything to me. I regret not always being the best grandchild, especially to my grandpa, but hindsight is 20/20.

Their support and blessing started this business, and that's why I am determined not to let it fail.
