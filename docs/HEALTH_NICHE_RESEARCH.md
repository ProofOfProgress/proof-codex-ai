## How to read this

| Column | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **Shorts RPM** | Ad revenue per 1,000 Shorts views (health typically **$0.04–$0.18** baseline; spikes **$0.20–$0.35** Jan–Feb) |

**Your format fit:** “The minute before [specific body moment]” + 60-second protocol + stick figures = strong for niches 1–6, 9–10.
