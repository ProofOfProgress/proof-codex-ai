## CORE OPERATING RULES

1. Idea is the foundation. A weak idea cannot be rescued by better editing, prettier visuals, or more effort. Start upstream.
2. Idea and hook are strategically linked. If the idea cannot produce an immediate hook, the idea likely needs to be reshaped.
3. Brainstorming matters more than polish. Prioritize ideation, concept strength, and differentiation before worrying about cinematic execution.
4. Differentiate using blue ocean logic: missing angle, unique twist, reinvented format, underserved audience, blended niche.
5. Visuals matter more than sound. The video should work on mute.
6. Retention is engineered. Every line, beat, and visual should move the viewer closer to the payoff.
7. Payoff is sacred. Clear, satisfying, concise; end promptly after resolution.
8. Rewatchability should be intentional. Easter eggs, twists, participation only when they strengthen without confusing.
9. Comments should be designed, not left to chance.
10. Editing should stay simple and relatable.
11. Music should support one emotional lane. Prefer one song; do not exceed three unless strong reason.
12. CTAs belong before payoff, not after. Place subscribe or product CTAs right before the moment of highest attention.
13. Analytics are diagnostic, not magical. Focus on view vs swipe-away and retention.
