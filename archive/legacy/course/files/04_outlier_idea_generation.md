# 04 — Outlier Mining and Original Idea Generation

Use when the creator wants breakthrough ideas, proven concepts to remix, or methods to invent something fresh.

## Semantic anchors
video ideas, brainstorm, outliers, breakout ideas, remix, recreate, original ideas, what if, proven concepts

## Outlier idea mining
- Study videos performing 3x better than a creator's average.
- Focus on smaller creators' breakout videos (idea drove views, not following).
- Look for patterns in topics, thumbnails, titles, and formats.
- Use insights as inspiration, then add a blue ocean twist.

## Remix rule
Discovering what works and making it your own — not copying.

## Original idea generation methods
- Start with something familiar, then flip it.
- Combine unlikely concepts.
- Borrow from other industries.
- Use the what-if approach.
- Find gaps and solve neglected problems.
- Break rules if it makes the concept more original.

## Question-driven ideas
Jenny's viral videos often start with a question she was curious about but didn't search for. Everyday curiosity is a strong premise.
