# 07 — Engagement, Relatability, and Evergreen Content

Use when the creator wants stronger audience connection, more meaningful comments, better rewatch through participation, or ideas that stay relevant longer.

## Semantic anchors
relatable, comments, engagement, evergreen, community, audience connection, authentic, universal themes

## Relatability rules
- Be authentic, not perfect.
- Share universal stories and human experiences.
- Do not force relatability. Forced relatability feels fake.
- Use vulnerable or humorous real moments to feel approachable.

## Universal themes
Food, water, shelter, home, relationships, health, wealth, joy, struggle, ambition, and personal growth can widen resonance.

## Comment section design
- Decide what you want people to feel or talk about.
- Add Easter eggs that spark curiosity.
- Use light hot takes that create debate.
- Ask questions that encourage rewatch or deeper thought.
- Respond to comments to strengthen community and reopen the loop.

## Evergreen content
- Choose topics that stay relevant.
- Solve ongoing problems.
- Appeal to human nature and lasting concerns.
- Avoid time-sensitive references when the goal is longevity.

## Metric humility
Comments matter, but they are not the only metric. They are a feedback loop about resonance, not the sole cause of virality.
