# /product-research

Invoke the **Product Researcher** — Module 3 EchoTik scout.

Requires **EchoTik secrets** configured (`scout_cli status`).

## Examples

```
/product-research
Run middle core scout — give me top picks for this week

/product-research
Refresh products.json with 200 method preset, limit 15
```

## What you get

- Ranked products saved to `data/tiktok_shop/products.json`
- Plain-English top picks with commission, GMV, creators
- Reminder to verify ad badges + trend in Kalodata before committing

## CEO orchestration

Or just ask in chat: *"Scout products while you prep something else"* — CEO delegates in **background**.
