# Cosy aesthetic — Soft Continuity visual identity

**Soft Continuity** should feel like a warm room at 10pm — not a fluorescent office bathroom.

## One-line visual pitch

Stick-figure self-help in **warm domestic spaces**: lamp glow, rainy windows, couch blankets, tea mugs, quiet beds. Same *Minute Before* format; softer world.

## Palette

| Role | Hex | Use |
|------|-----|-----|
| Wall cream | `#F5EFE6` | Default room wash |
| Floor warm | `#E8DFD4` | Wood / carpet tone |
| Line art | `#1A1A1A` | Stick figures, outlines (softer than pure black) |
| Lamp glow | `#F2D98A` | Floor lamp, candle hints |
| Sage accent | `#9DB8A0` | Plant, blanket edge |
| Terracotta | `#C9A08A` | Couch, knit throw |
| Rain glass | `#A8B8C8` | Overcast / Sunday window |
| Night sky | `#2A3040` | 3am window (moon still soft, not horror) |

Channel banner/profile may stay dark void (`#0B0D10`, `#8EB8FF`) — **video frames are cosy bright**.

## Set dressing (minimal ChainsFR rules)

Only what the line mentions. Prefer:

- **Couch + throw** — Sunday dread, can't move, texting guilt
- **Bed + blanket** — 3am, can't sleep, low-spoon mornings
- **Rain window** — grey day, overthinking, waiting
- **Warm lamp** — breathing, grounding, "still here"
- **Mug / tea** — small wins, morning ritual, one thing you can do
- **Kitchen nook** — eating nothing all day, standing at counter

**Avoid as default:** office desks, bathroom stalls, calendar posters, fluorescent vibes. Use only when the script literally says work/office/bathroom.

## Audio / CapCut mood

- VO: calm, slightly slower — like talking to someone on your couch
- Music: lo-fi ambient, soft rain, no drops (YouTube Audio Library)
- No corporate whoosh SFX

## Topic alignment (cosy ≠ low RPM)

Cosy domestic mental health still attracts therapy-app advertisers. Hooks move from **office** to **home minutes**:

| Old (office) | Cosy replacement |
|--------------|------------------|
| Office bathroom cry | Can't leave the couch / canceling plans from bed |
| Pre-meeting panic | Before replying to a text under a blanket |
| Sunday work email | Sunday phone check from the couch, rain outside |
| Performance review | Before opening a message you're dreading (phone, sofa) |

## Anti-slop

- No Pinterest "hygge quote" slides — stick figures **doing** one protocol
- No same couch every frame — change lamp/window/prop per beat
- Warm ≠ childish — still first-person, specific, adult loneliness
