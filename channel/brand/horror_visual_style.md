# PERIPHERAL — visual DNA

## Logo / hero motif
- **One huge freaky eye** — macro iris, veins, wet reflection, staring **directly at camera**
- High contrast: black sclera edges, cold highlight on cornea, crimson accent in iris only
- Use in hook or final beat — not wallpaper on every shot

## Palette
- Black `#0A0A0A`, bone white `#E8E4DC`, cold steel `#4A5568`, dried blood `#6B0F1A` (accent — symbols, light spill, not gore spray)
- Film grain, harsh contrast, underexposed interiors, single hard light source

## Aesthetic contrast (content + future merch)
- **Sacred eye** × **biker raw** — leather texture, chain, worn denim, industrial rust, fog forest
- Not mall-goth, not cosy, not anime

## Subjects (faceless until scare)
- Forest stone circles, hooded silhouettes, basement traps, industrial rooms
- Ritual tools on stone, chains, masks — **no explicit violence on screen**
- Security cam, POV witness, slow pan to empty chair
- Final beat: movement at **edge of frame** or eye filling lens

## Implied gore (critical)
- Show: anticipation, tools, reactions, aftermath, off-screen sound
- Never: open wounds, explicit act, blood spray, step-by-step harm
- Aftermath OK: blood on snow (no body), empty circle, steam, dropped mask

## Motion (I2V)
- Slow dread drift → false calm hold → snap to periphery scare
- Chanting scenes: subtle fire flicker, figures still, camera creeps
- Final beat: fast lunge from edge of frame or iris dilation fill

## Never
- Stick figures, cosy lamp, self-help tone, bright daylight cheer
- Explicit gore, real crime, identifiable real victims
- Text baked into image, logos, watermarks

## Caption safe zone
- Bottom 40% clear for ASS burn-in
