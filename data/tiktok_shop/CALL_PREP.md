# Call prep — affiliate + accounts (read on the call)

**Rule:** Ask everything. **Buy nothing** on the call.

---

## Account buying (if he sells or recommends)

1. Does it already have **TikTok Shop Product Marketplace** — or only followers?
2. **1,000 vs 5,000** followers — what do I need for US affiliate full access?
3. Are followers **real**? Show **views on old posts**, not just follower count.
4. **Refund or replacement** if banned in the first 30 days?
5. I get **email + password + phone** day one — you never log in again?
6. **ID verification** — must be **my** name? What if already verified as someone else?
7. OK to log in from **my home phone** — no VPN required?
8. How many **shop videos per day** is safe week one?

---

## Business / tools

9. **EchoTik** enough for product research or need Charm/Kalodata?
10. Best **niches** for new affiliates (beauty, home, gadgets)?
11. Realistic **first-month earnings** with 5K + 3–5 clips/day? (Get a range.)
12. **One account** to start or three immediately?

---

## Red flags — leave

- Today-only discount  
- No refund on ban  
- Can't demo Marketplace  
- Under **$50** for "5K account"  
- "Post 30/day immediately"  
- Wants ongoing access to your account
