## Key definitions (owner note)

- **Product image** — the product on a **blank/white background** (isolated). Example: Kalodata listing photo with white background.
- **Reference image** — the product in a **natural setting** so the AI understands **real-world size/scale**. Without this, generated size can be wrong (product looks huge or tiny).

In prompts to the ChatGPT builder: **product images** = isolated; **reference images** = in-context setting. Reference goes **leftmost** when both are used.

## Higgsfield settings (when not using sheet only)

- Model: **NanoBanana**
- Size: **9:16**
- Resolution: **2K** (1K ok to experiment)
- Batch: **1/4** image generations (rarely **2** — e.g. human likeness, large furniture/couch where scale matters)

## Auto-prompter sheet fields

Fill in:

1. **Reference images** (count + upload — in-context product shots)  
2. **Product images** (count + upload — isolated white-background)  
3. **Amenity images** (optional props/staging elements)  

Plus: product niche (beauty, supplement, furniture, etc.), product quantity, product orientation, amenity quantity, staging color, staging surface, amenities text.

Sheet **auto-generates the prompt** → copy → paste into Higgsfield.

Supplement / beauty / furniture products: read niche-specific notes on the sheet.
