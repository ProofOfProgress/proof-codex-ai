## What actually matters for bottom-of-funnel (BOF)

| Myth | Reality |
|------|---------|
| Video length = “quality” | **No** — not the main BOF quality signal |
| On-screen text / audio = this violation type | **No** — for *this* metric, CTR + sales matter more |
| Bottom-of-funnel is “bad” / getting removed | **No** — BOF is core to the funnel and conversions |

### CTR target

- **Click-through rate (CTR)** = viewers who see the video → click the **orange shopping cart**
- Aim **≥ 5% CTR** for **consistent sales**
- Track in TikTok ads / Shop analytics; optimize hooks and product-offer fit

---

## Misinformation violations (June 8, 2026)

**Root cause:** your video promises a **sale / price / discount / coupon / free shipping** that is **not true when the viewer clicks** — especially while brands run **GMV Max** ad spend.

**Example shown:** [@david.diy88](https://www.tiktok.com/@david.diy88) — video says **“50% off”** but the product is **not always** 50% off. When the offer changes, viewers expect the old deal → **misinformation** → violations / account issues.

**This is not “BOF is banned.”** It's **lies and stale promo language**.

### Words to avoid (on-screen caption + spoken/audio)

Avoid these **for now** (promotions change daily):

- **sale**
- **price**
- **discount**
- **coupon**
- **free shipping**

Also keep avoiding **specific percentages** (Module 6) — e.g. “50% off”, “30% off”.

### Safer urgency patterns (Module 6 — still OK if true-ish)

- “Literally pennies to clear extra stock”
- Date windows (“June 8–10th…”)
- “Violently discounted” *(wording without a numeric % — confirm on group calls if still safe)*
- Pain-point hooks without quoting exact price or shipping terms

When in doubt → **group call** or owner update.

## What BOF is (and isn't)

- **Bottom-of-funnel** = direct product/conversion creative — **required** part of marketing
- TikTok is **not** going to remove the funnel; BOF drives conversions
- Middle-of-funnel vs BOF debates miss the point — **misinformation** is the fixable problem

## Checklist before post

1. **Module 1 QC** — zero visual/posting don’ts (`module_01_read_before_anything.md`)
2. **No misinformation words** — sale, price, discount, coupon, free shipping; no false % off
3. **Caption/prompt** matches **current** offer (or stays vague on price/shipping)
4. **Monitor CTR** — target ≥ 5% on BOF tests
5. **After group calls** — apply owner’s latest word list / prompt rules
