STRATEGY
Beauty Product(s)

Single, duo, and multi-product beauty prompts.

Broken down by type of beauty product and/or bundle. A generic template is first for single beauty product videos. The more images you provide Nano Banana Pro, the higher your error rate / inconsistency.

The following is assumed:
- 1 Reference Image of the product (Image 1 | Most Left Image)
- 1 Isolated Product Image of the product (Image 2 | Most Right or Middle Image)
- n more Isolated Product Image(s) if applicable (Image n | Most Left Image[s])

For multiple of a product, put this sentence last: “Put X of the product from image 2 side by side.” (Recommended to only do 2 side by side, unless trio or larger bundle.)

Beauty Product | 1 Product

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the product from image 2. Put the product from image 2 on a dark brown wood makeup vanity table with nameless makeup in the background with natural lighting. (photo realistic, matching lighting)

Beauty Product | 1 Product | Duo

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the product from image 2. Put the product from image 2 on a dark brown wood makeup vanity table with nameless makeup in the background with natural lighting. Put 2 of the product from image 2 side by side. (photo realistic, matching lighting)

Beauty Product | Multi Product

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the products from images 2 - 4. Put the exact products from images 2 - 4 side by side on a dark brown wood makeup vanity table with nameless makeup in the background with natural lighting. (photo realistic, matching lighting)
