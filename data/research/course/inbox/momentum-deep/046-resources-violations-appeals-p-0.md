Prompt 1 | General TikTok Shop Violation Appeal

The all-purpose appeal prompt for any violation.

Add a screenshot of your violation to ChatGPT along with the prompt below. It will write an appeal under 500 characters (as TikTok requires) and tell you exactly which screenshots to attach. Or just use the Violation Appeal Agent™ to do it all automatically.
Copy
Please provide me with a violation appeal for the attached screenshot that is under 500 characters, respectful but stern. The appeal should directly address the violation shown in the screenshot and firmly state that the content, product link, and product representation were accurate, compliant, and not in violation of the alleged issue.

Do not state or imply that I may have committed the violation. Do not use language such as “if I made a mistake,” “I may have,” “possibly,” or anything that admits or suggests fault. The appeal should confidently deny the alleged issue while remaining professional.

The appeal should close by requesting a manual review of the violation and complete reinstatement of the product link on the violated video once the violation is withdrawn.

After the appeal, provide a separate list of screenshots I should attach to support the appeal. Before listing the screenshots, include this exact statement:
“Please note: The screenshots listed below are optional, but recommended. Not all of the screenshots listed below need to be linked, but all are viable and will be beneficial to getting your violation withdrawn.”

The screenshot list should include items such as:
1. Screenshot of the violation notice
2. Screenshot of the product clearly shown in the video
3. Screenshot of the linked TikTok Shop product listing
4. Screenshot of the product in my TikTok Shop showcase
5. Screenshot of the product title/name
6. Screenshot comparing the product in the video to the product listing
7. Screenshot of any seller-provided product images or details that match the product shown
8. Screenshot of the specific product claim, price, discount, or sale percentage shown in the video, if relevant
9. Screenshot of the product listing showing the matching claim, price, discount, or sale percentage, if relevant
10. Timestamped proof that the product details, price, discount, or sale percentage matched at the time of posting, if available

End the attachment section with a short sentence explaining which images should not be attached. For this violation, do not recommend attaching unrelated screenshots, unrelated product listings, blurry product images, screenshots that show a different product, screenshots that show a different current price or sale percentage than the one shown in the video, or ID verification images unless TikTok specifically requests identity verification.
