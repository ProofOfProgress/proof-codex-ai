Prompt 2 | Product Mismatch / Wrong Product

When they claim the video product doesn’t match the listing.

Add a screenshot of your violation to ChatGPT along with the prompt below. It will write an appeal under 500 characters (as TikTok requires) and tell you exactly which screenshots to attach. Or just use the Violation Appeal Agent™ to do it all automatically.
Copy
Please provide me with a violation appeal for the attached screenshot that is under 500 characters, respectful but stern. The violation appears to claim that the product shown in my video does not match the linked TikTok Shop product. The appeal should firmly state that the product displayed in the video accurately matches the linked product listing and that there was no inaccurate representation.

Do not state or imply that I may have committed the violation. The appeal should close by requesting a manual review of the violation and complete reinstatement of the product link on the violated video once the violation is withdrawn.

After the appeal, provide a separate list of screenshots I should attach to support the appeal. Before listing the screenshots, include this exact statement:
“Please note: The screenshots listed below are optional, but recommended. Not all of the screenshots listed below need to be linked, but all are viable and will be beneficial to getting your violation withdrawn.”

The screenshot list should include items such as:
1. Screenshot of the violation notice
2. Screenshot of the product clearly shown in the video
3. Screenshot of the linked TikTok Shop product listing
4. Screenshot comparing the video product to the listing product
5. Screenshot of the product in my TikTok Shop showcase
6. Screenshot of the product title/name
7. Screenshot of any seller-provided product images that match the product shown

End the attachment section with a short sentence explaining which images should not be attached. For this violation, do not recommend attaching unrelated ID verification screenshots, unrelated product listings, or screenshots of a different product variation unless that exact variation is what appears in the video.
