Prompt 4 | Product Not Clearly Shown

When they say the linked product wasn’t clearly shown in the video.

Add a screenshot of your violation to ChatGPT along with the prompt below. It will write an appeal under 500 characters (as TikTok requires) and tell you exactly which screenshots to attach. Or just use the Violation Appeal Agent™ to do it all automatically.
Copy
Please provide me with a violation appeal for the attached screenshot that is under 500 characters, respectful but stern. The violation appears to claim that the product link was improper because the linked product was not clearly shown, featured, or connected to the video. The appeal should firmly state that the linked product was clearly represented in the video and that the product link accurately matched the content shown.

Do not state or imply that I may have committed the violation. The appeal should close by requesting a manual review of the violation and complete reinstatement of the product link on the violated video once the violation is withdrawn.

After the appeal, provide a separate list of screenshots I should attach to support the appeal. Before listing the screenshots, include this exact statement:
“Please note: The screenshots listed below are optional, but recommended. Not all of the screenshots listed below need to be linked, but all are viable and will be beneficial to getting your violation withdrawn.”

The screenshot list should include items such as:
1. Screenshot of the violation notice
2. Screenshot of the product clearly visible in the video
3. Screenshot of the linked TikTok Shop product listing
4. Screenshot showing the product name/title
5. Screenshot showing the product’s appearance matching the video
6. Screenshot of the product in my TikTok Shop showcase
7. Screenshot of the video frame where the product is being used, held, displayed, or referenced
8. Screenshot of seller-provided product images that match the product in the video

End the attachment section with a short sentence explaining which images should not be attached. For this violation, do not recommend attaching screenshots where the product is blurry, blocked, barely visible, unrelated to the product link, or showing a different product than the one linked in the video.
