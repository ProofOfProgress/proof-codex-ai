Appeal Denied? Escalate to Live Support

If you get an appeal denied, escalate to a live support agent.

1
In TikTok Shop for Creator, tap your profile picture (top right).
2
Go to Help Center → Chat with us.
3
Spam the support ticket until you get a solid live agent who can really look through your case and help get you unbanned.
Yes, this is annoying. But there is about a 1 in 5 chance the violation gets revoked this way. Just keep opening support tickets until you get a SOLID rep.
