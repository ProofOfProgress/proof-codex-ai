STRATEGY
Supplement Product(s)

Prompts for supplements, with and without amenities.

This is broken down by different types of supplements. A generic version of the prompt for simpler images is provided first before involving amenities such as pills or gummies.

The following is assumed:
- 1 Reference Image of the product (Image 1 | Most Left Image)
- 1 Isolated Product Image of the product (Image 2 | Most Right or Middle Image)
- 1 Isolated Amenity Image from the product if applicable (Image 3 | Most Left Image)

For multiple of a product, put this sentence last / after the second sentence: “Put X of the product from image 2 side by side.” (Recommended to only do 2 side by side, unless it is a trio or larger bundle, to maintain good text on product.)

Supplement Product | No Amenities Involved

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the product from image 2. Put the product from image 2 on a cream colored marble kitchen table with natural lighting. (photo realistic, matching lighting)

Supplement Product | Amenities Involved

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the products from images 2 - 3. Put the product from image 2 on a cream colored marble kitchen table with natural lighting. Put 3 of the product from image 3 scattered around the product from image 2. The product from image 3 is 1/4th of an inch in size. (photo realistic, matching lighting)

Supplement Product | Multiple Products | No Amenities Involved

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the product from image 2. Put the product from image 2 on a cream colored marble kitchen table with natural lighting. Put 2 of the product from image 2 side by side. (photo realistic, matching lighting)

Supplement Product | Multiple Products | Amenities Involved

Copy
Only use image 1 as a reference for the scale and dimensions of the products from images 2 - 3. Put the product from image 2 on a cream colored marble kitchen table with natural lighting. Put 2 of the product from image 2 side by side. Put 5 of the product from image 3 scattered around the products from image 2. The product from image 3 is 1/4th of an inch in size. (photo realistic, matching lighting)
