Editing Process
1
Open the TikTok Studio app.
2
Trim the start and end so there is no dead space.
3
Add the red text hook on top. Keep it simple, based on what the creator says.
4
Add the white text hook on the bottom. Watch the video: if there's a coupon code, use "Code: XXX". If not, use urgency ("Ends tonight", "6 hours left", "2 hours left", etc.). Switch it up often.
Technical Guidelines
⚠️ Master Drive link still needs to be added here.

Once done, upload to the Google Drive master folder. The UGC creator uploads raw footage to "RAW CLIPS ⚠️". The editor edits the clips in TikTok Studio, saves them, and uploads them back to the drive in the "UPLOAD READY ✅" folder.

Quality Control — Version A (Standard BOF)
•
Red background, white text on top
•
White background, black text on the bottom
•
No dead space before or after
•
Placed directly in the center of the screen
•
Enough space left to see all parts of the video (good positioning / text size)
Quality Control — Version B (No Sound + Faceless)
•
White background with red text "BUY 1 GET 1 🔥"
•
Placed directly in the center of the screen (try not to block the product)
•
Enough space left to see all parts of the video
•
One of the approved sounds added (save these to your TikTok Studio)

Google Drive Training Time
⚠️ Master Drive link still needs to be added here.

Download the videos from the master Drive folder (inside the EDITOR TRAINING SECTION) into TikTok Studio, edit them (no dead space, correct text as instructed above), then upload into the "READY TRAINING ✅" section inside editor training. Rename the edited files with "YOUR NAME + NUMBER OF TRIES." For example, if your name is Joe and it's your first time editing these clips, name them "Joe 1 A" and "Joe 1 B" for the first and second clips.
