Inconsistent / Irrelevant Product Promotion
Copy
I am writing to formally appeal a violation I received for "Irrelevant Product Promotion" on my TikTok Shop account. I believe this violation was issued in error, as the content in question accurately represents and promotes the product listed in my shop.
This is the hardest one to appeal, especially for AI BOF. Best defense is prevention: add the product name into the video (then move it off-screen).
