Get from 0 to 5K followers and into the Shop programs.

Important Regarding Growing Accounts
It is highly recommended that you follow this checklist in addition to just spam posting bubble-wrap videos:
1. Use a new email and warm up the account. When opening a new TikTok account, use a new email, and before posting, scroll the For You page for 10 to 20 minutes. Preferably give yourself 1 to 2 days solely for scrolling the For You page. Follow some accounts, comment on some videos, and follow some bubble wrap creators.
2. Brand the account. Make your profile picture and bio branded to bubble wrap, like the accounts who use the sound provided.
3. Start with a journey video, not a bubble wrap video. Your first video should be a "Here to start my journey" video, not a bubble wrap video. This disrupts the algorithm on your account, so the bubble wrap videos you post after will not look like spam. We have tested this and it works the best.
4. Break up the bubble wrap. Between every 4 to 5 bubble wrap videos, one video should be a different type of video:
• You just playing a game
• A random video from your camera roll
• A new "How to Start Your Journey" video
• A Fortnite clip
• Or whatever it may be
Every 4 to 5 bubble wrap videos, post something random to disrupt the algorithm.
5. Get into the Creator Pilot program before running ads. Before running ads, make sure you actually get your account into the Creator Pilot program. It gives you access to TikTok Shop features but limits you to only 3 shoppable posts per day. We want to opt in here before running ads because it prevents the "growing too quickly" violation you would get if you run ads before getting into the pilot program.
Note: What we have recently seen, and it is normal, is that sometimes people get into the pilot program, then reach 5,000 followers, and still have to go through a 30-day period of only being able to post 3 videos per day. That is fine and normal. Just make sure your videos are high quality and be patient.
Fastest path: The best approach, if you want to start making sales and commissions as soon as possible, is to just buy an account, which I highly recommend everyone do.
🚨 IMPORTANT: Do NOT run Promote ads to get to 1K. Staying organic to 1K gives you a higher chance of getting into the Pilot program, which boosts your chances of getting into the 5K Shop Affiliate program a LOT more.
