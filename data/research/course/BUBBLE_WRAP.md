## Video format (owner + samples, June 2026)

### Frame 1 — bubble-wrapped subject + hook

- **Visual:** Whatever you design, **fully covered in bubble wrap** (bathroom, duck, suit, random object, etc.)
- **Hook text** (top performers use one of these patterns):
  - `bubble wrap asmr>>>>`
  - `[subject] bubble wrap ASMR` (sometimes **ASMR** capitalized)
  - `perfect for people with ADHD` *(very common on winners)*

### Frame 2 — interaction → pop (when using 2-slide posts)

From course + viral sound page — map each action to a pop size:

| Action | Pop label | Explosion emojis |
|--------|-----------|------------------|
| Pause / Press | pop | 💥 (small) |
| Share | loud / strange pop | 💥💥 |
| Follow | giant / weird pop | 💥💥💥 |
| Comment | big pop | 💥💥 |

**Rule:** Use **explosion emojis only** (not 🫧) — **1 to 3** 💥 depending on **how big the pop** is supposed to be.

Other hooks that still work: `Pop The Bubble Wrap`, `Great for ADHD`, labeled bubble-wrap scenes (bathroom items, etc.).
