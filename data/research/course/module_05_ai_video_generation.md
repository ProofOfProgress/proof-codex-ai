## Kling 2.6 settings (Higgsfield)

| Setting | Value |
|--------|--------|
| Model | **Kling 2.6** |
| Preset | **GENERAL** |
| Duration | **5 seconds** |
| Audio | **Off** |
| Enhance | **Off** |
| Image input | Upload Module 04 image → aspect/ratio goes to **Auto** (that’s fine) |
| Output (typical) | **1080p**, **5.0s**, **9:16** |

**Steps:**

1. Open Higgsfield → **Video** → **Create Video**
2. Choose **GENERAL** preset → select **Kling 2.6**
3. Turn **Audio** off
4. Set duration to **5s**
5. Upload the product image you generated in **Module 04** (from Downloads / your image folder)
6. Set **Enhance** to **Off**
7. Leave image sizing on **Auto**
8. Paste the **all-purpose video prompt** (below) into the prompt field
9. Click **Generate** — wait for the 5s clip

---

## All-purpose video prompt (copy/paste — do not change)

Instructor says this prompt **almost never needs to change** and has a **super low error rate**. If something looks wrong, the problem is usually the **image** (Module 04), not the prompt.

```
Arc Camera Shot from left to right, handheld but naturally stabilized. Motion is smooth and organic with gentle human drift, not shaky. Keep all of the products stationary and in the center of the shot.
```

**Do not overcomplicate video generation.** Editing is covered in the next module.
