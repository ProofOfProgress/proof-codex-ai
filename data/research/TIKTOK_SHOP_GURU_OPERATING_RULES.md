# TikTok Shop guru operating rules (Jon Reiter seller course)

## Business model
Primary path is TikTok Shop **Seller**, not Affiliate Creator. Seller can start with **zero followers** on the official linked TikTok account. Affiliate path requires ~5,000 real followers.

## Product research order
Discover winners on TikTok search and shop tab first. Validate timing, sales, and creators in analytics (Kalodata in course; EchoTik in our stack). Only then create or twist the product. Copy early winners (1k+ units, low reviews) or twist stale winners.

## Launch sequence
Complete seller signup, Printify connection, and official TikTok link before heavy marketing. Warm every listing with **3–5 in-house videos/day** before sending affiliate samples at scale.

## Affiliate commission
Launch open/target collaboration at **~30%** for new shops, not low commission. Frame creator outreach in **dollars per order**, not percentages only.

## Affiliate onboarding KPI
North-star metric: **videos posted per sample sent**. Target 2–5+; top operators reach 7–20. Never auto-approve sample requests — vet content quality first.

## Creator relationships
Send target invite **and** TikTok DM. Move proven creators to text/WhatsApp. Weekly Notion creator brief with hooks, winning examples, dos/don'ts, and fresh proof.

## Retainers
No retainer until **4–5 test videos** prove performance. Market standard ~30 vids/$1k; prefer smaller packages (16–20 vids/$400) plus boosted commission.

## GMV Max timing
Start GMV Max only with **25–50 videos/week** in circulation or clear organic winners. Example POD test: ~$200/day budget, target ROI ~5. Refresh creative constantly.

## POD operations
Edit listings in **Printify**, not TikTok Seller Center (one-way sync). Keep ~$1,000 working capital for Printify charges before TikTok payout. Order samples from your own live listing.

## Violations and appeals
Draft appeals with AI. Use account health → violation details → live chat for expedited tickets. Include claims compliance in creator briefs.

## Bot factory alignment
Use EchoTik scout for research validation. Kling + loop-clip for in-house faceless seller content. Affiliate DM/samples/briefs remain human until built. Do not assume pure 0-follower affiliate marketplace access.
