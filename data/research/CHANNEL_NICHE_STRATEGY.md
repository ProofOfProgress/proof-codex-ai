## Locked hierarchy

```
AI / Tech
    └── AI product reviews
            └── Honest 30-second verdicts on real AI products
```

| Level | Name |
|-------|------|
| **Big** | AI / Tech |
| **Sub** | **AI product reviews** |
| **Sub-sub** | **Real product, honest verdict** — Pay / Skip / Wait in ~30s |

**One-line pitch:** I test AI products so you don't waste money — no hype, clear verdict.

**Why this lane wins:**
- AI products never stop launching → infinite topics

---

## Short format (every upload)

| Beat | What happens |
|------|----------------|
| **Hook (0–3s)** | "Everyone's using [Product]. I tested it." |
| **Body (3–22s)** | One real flaw OR surprise — price, privacy, quality, lock-in, watermark |
| **Verdict (22–28s)** | **Pay / Skip / Wait** + one sentence why |
| **CTA (28–30s)** | "Follow — next AI product tomorrow." |

**Rules:**
- **Real products only** — named tools people can look up (ChatGPT, InVideo, NotebookLM, etc.)
- **One product per Short** — no listicles
- **Original opinion** — not a landing-page rewrite
- **Sincere first** — say Skip when it's bad; say Pay when it's actually good
- 25–35s · InVideo twin · captions on · synthetic media disclosed

---

## Sponsor / affiliate path (future — not day one)

**Now:** Unsponsored honest reviews only. Build trust + watch time.

**Later (when inbound or outbound deals exist):**

| Rule | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Disclose every time** | "#ad" / "paid partnership" in video + description — YouTube + FTC |
| **Sponsored ≠ automatic Pay** | Paid integrations still need a real verdict; owner approves script |
| **Separate queue** | Organic reviews vs sponsored — never blur without disclosure |
| **Affiliate links OK** | Description only; mention if link earns commission |
| **Trust > short money** | One shady sponsor spot kills the "honest reviewer" brand |

**Monetization ladder:**
1. Shorts ad RPM (tech/product category)
2. Affiliate links on tools you actually recommend
3. Sponsored Shorts / integrations (disclosed)
4. Long-form deep dives + course ("how to pick AI tools") once catalog is deep
