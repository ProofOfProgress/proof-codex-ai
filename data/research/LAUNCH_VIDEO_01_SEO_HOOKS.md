## 2. Hook angles (pick one for line 1 + title)

**Winner (recommended):**
> You blinked at the mirror — your reflection blinked one second later.

**Alternates (A/B later):**
1. My reflection blinked, but I didn't.
2. The mirror showed me blinking a second too late.
3. I saw my reflection move independently.

**Hook rules (Jenny × horror):**
- Speak hook in **first 3 seconds** (Shorts SEO weights spoken opening)
- **No** "scary story #12" / "hey guys"
- **Singular "you"** — micro-story, not lecture
- Title = hook (front-load first 40 chars) + 🔊 volume warning
